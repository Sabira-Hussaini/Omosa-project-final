Our partners

OMOSA is a full-service eCommerce agency partnering with
fashion, lifestyle, and design brands.
We bring together strategic thinking, creative direction, and
operational clarity, to help brands grow, and keep growing.
Founded by Axel Corton after years scaling brands from the inside,
OMOSA is made up of experts in eCommerce direction, UX/UI
design, Paid Media, CRM, and creative strategy.
We’ve worked brand-side and agency-side. We know what it takes
to hit targets, launch on time, and scale efficiently.
We don’t believe in one-off projects or short-term hacks.
We embed ourselves into your business, collaborate with your
team, and take ownership from day one.
Our method is simple:
→ Immerse ourselves in your brand
→ Move fast, test smart, stay focused
→ Build partnerships that last, quarter after quarter, year after year
